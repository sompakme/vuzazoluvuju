Andrade Posts Photo Of Sleeping Charlotte Flair

Charlotte Flair is on a lot of fans’ minds right now. WWE wrote Lana out of the picture on RAW, and Asuka needs a new partner to face Nia Jax and Shayna Baszler for the WWE Women’s Tag Team Titles. This could open the door for The Queen’s return.

WWE is keeping very tight-lipped about Charlotte Flair’s possible return at TLC.

Andrade surprised fans on the morning of WWE TLC. He posted a photo of himself in bed with Charlotte Flair, and she was very unaware that he took this photo; because she was still sleeping soundly. Flair was also sleeping in her earrings and it was hard to ignore the wrist bands she was sporting too.

Good morning! No more my queen is now my sleeping beauty. @MsCharlotteWWE #Mami is kidding you will always be my queen and the one who reigns over all.

Andrade posted the same photo and caption to Instagram. He did not tag any location, so have no idea how close The Queen is to Tampa.

A lot of fans are expecting to see Charlotte Flair at WWE TLC. If she shows up, at least we’ll know she had a good night’s sleep.

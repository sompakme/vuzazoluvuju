Kalyaan Dhev's Transofrmation Amid Divorce Rumours

The rumours of Sreeja and Kalyaan Dhev’s divorce intensified when the former changed her social media handle name from Sreeja Kalyaan to Sreeja Konidela. She further stopped following Kalyaan on Instagram who is also the father of her younger daughter Navishka.

Amid the strong rumours of the couple’s split up, here’s Kalyaan Dhev getting busy for his next project. The actor posted a picture flaunting his huge deltoid muscles. His transformation left netizens spellbound. While the majority of netizens lauded his efforts at the gym, a few were curious to ask about his marital status.

“When people tell you, you can’t do it. Just laugh and tell them, “watch me do it”, wrote Kalyan as a caption for his inspiring picture. He is seen posing in the gym in a blue tank top. On the work front, Kalyaan will be seen in Kinnerasani, directed by Ramana Teja. Written by Desharaj, the film is produced by SRT Entertainments.

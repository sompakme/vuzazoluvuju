Rajendra Prasad's Look in Sasanasabha

Versatile actor Rajendra Prasad is known for choosing different characters. He has come up with an interesting character in his forthcoming film ‘Sasanasabha’. Rajendra Prasad essays the role of Narayana Swamy, a patriot and a people’s leader in the film. On the eve of 76th Independence Day, the makers of the film have unveiled Rajendra Prasad’s character reveal poster. The movie is directed by Venu Madikanti, who is known for his work on Venkatapuram.

Recently, the motion poster with captivating images of the Constitution book and legislative assembly as well as thumping background score was presented by the film’s creators. The striking BGM was composed by KGF and Salaar fame Ravi Basrur and it was received quite well.

Rajendra Prasad plays Narayana Swamy who would be a Member of the Legislative Assembly. He will play his finest character in this film, as he has done in previous roles. This character will be the highlight of this pan-India political film. The lead actor in “Sasanasabha” will be Indra Sena, while the lead lady will be Aishwarya Raj Bhakuni.

Besides Rajendra Prasad, the film’s cast include Sonia Agarwal, of 7G Brindavan Colony fame, Prudhvi Raj, Anish Kuruvilla, Jabardasth Apparao and Amit, among others. Hebah Patel is doing a special song in this film.

The movie seems to be a spicy political thriller that will appeal to contemporary culture based on its imagery and subject matter.

Lisa Rinna’s Early Life and Career

Born in Newport Beach, California, Lisa Rinna began her acting career in the late 1980s. She first gained recognition for her role as Billie Reed on the NBC daytime soap opera “Days of Our Lives”. Her performance earned her critical acclaim and paved the way for more significant roles in the future.

Breakthrough with Melrose Place

In 1996, Rinna joined the cast of the Fox primetime soap opera “Melrose Place,” playing the character of Taylor McBride. This role further boosted her popularity and significantly contributed to her growing wealth.

Reality Television and Beyond

Rinna’s career took a new turn when she joined the reality television world. She first appeared in “Dancing with the Stars” and later became a main cast member of “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills”. These appearances not only increased her visibility but also added a substantial amount to her net worth.

Entrepreneurial Ventures

Apart from acting, Rinna has also ventured into business. She owns a clothing boutique called Belle Gray and has launched her own clothing line on QVC, both of which have contributed to her wealth.

Endorsements and Other Ventures

Pisces forecast for Sunday July 14, 2024

    Your Weekly Love Focus Horoscope: "Most people who ask for advice have already resolved to act as it pleases them." I'm starting with the words of Kahlil Gibran because they're so true (and so appropriate)! When we ask someone what they think, most of the time, we've already made our decision. We just want them to back our viewpoint. Which is why you're struggling to communicate with someone. You're wholeheartedly doing your best to advise them, but they keep repeating the same questions. If you listen this week, you'll have an enlightening realisation.

    July 13, 2024

    Your Weekly Horoscope: Pisceans famously 'go with the flow'. Like everything in life, this has its positives and negatives. Your natural adaptability means that although you're not contradictory, you're not always consistent. You're not rebellious, but you don't like being told what to do. You're talented, but you've got weaknesses too. The coming astrological Buck Moon encourages you to be more purposeful and direct. Good. If you focus, while keeping all these different attributes in mind, you've got every right to expect this week to be successful.

    July 12, 2024

    With Neptune's influence having such an impact, what does your weekend have in store? Maybe it's easier to look at what's unlikely to happen! You're not going to realise that your route to a dream is blocked. You're not going to find yourself struggling to prioritise. You won't face a difficult choice. Although there might be a few hidden surprises, you'll be more than able to cope. The key lies in your ability to intuit the thoughts of the people around you. If you listen, relationships, which support you into the future, will grow.

    July 11, 2024

    In situations of conflict there are no winners. And in affairs of the heart there are no losers. But even if we know that both those statements are true, we find it easy to forget. So we fight for what we believe to be right - without considering all the consequences. And we treat love as ownership - which can lead to unhappiness and loss. Yet when we genuinely give our hearts to someone (or something) our hearts can never be broken. As the planet of love links with your ruler, Neptune, today, your intuition is super-honed. Trust and follow it.

    July 10, 2024

    Your life isn't like the lives of the characters you see on TV or read about in books. No one has the same reactions and responses as you. And none of the storylines perfectly reflect your daily routine. Or mine. Or anyone else's. Why? Because writers inject extra drama to encourage us to watch! Could you be making an unfair comparison, and undermining your achievements? You're not living a fantasy. You're a human, doing your best. If you need more drama in your world, you can find it. But you could, instead, just relax.

    July 09, 2024

    When people speak considerately to one another they create a space in which amazing things can happen. Arguments can be resolved. Agreements can be reached. Resentments can be set aside. The challenge is being able to speak nicely when someone's not being nice. It's your ability to empathise that puts you into the role of peacemaker right now. Today brings the support (and courage) to address an awkward situation. With a kind-hearted approach you can encourage a conversation that moves towards agreement. Yay!

    July 08, 2024

    Goji berries are a superfood. Nightshade berries are poisonous. Fine. No one's going to throw any of the latter into a blender to make a smoothie! But they bear a close resemblance to one another. Which highlights the fact that some things look the same, but have very different consequences. Now, let's look at the choice you face. You're trying to differentiate between similar-looking scenarios. You feel as if one will be beneficial, and the other detrimental. But, unlike the berries, both options have their advantages. Relax.

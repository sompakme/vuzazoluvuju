Plotlines and Story

A Rich Lady is a romantic comedy that revolves around the love story of a young woman named Lee Soo-ah and a wealthy man named Han Ji-hoon. Lee Soo-ah is a cheerful and optimistic person who loves luxury and beauty. However, she has a hard time finding a stable job due to her extravagant personality. One day, she accidentally gets hired as a chauffeur for the Han family, a rich and powerful clan that owns a large entertainment company.

She soon meets Han Ji-hoon, the handsome and charismatic son of the family, who is also a famous actor under the stage name of Han Seo-joon. However, Lee Soo-ah does not recognize him, and she thinks he is just a spoiled and arrogant heir. Han Ji-hoon, on the other hand, is intrigued by Lee Soo-ah’s innocence and honesty, and he decides to hide his identity from her. As they spend more time together, they develop feelings for each other, but they also face many obstacles and misunderstandings along the way.

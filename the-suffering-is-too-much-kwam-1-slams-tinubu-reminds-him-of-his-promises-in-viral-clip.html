The Suffering Is Too Much: Kwam 1 Slams Tinubu, Reminds Him of His Promises in Viral Clip

  • Veteran Nigerian Fuji artist Wasiu Ayinde Marshal, aka Kwam 1, recently joined other Nigerians in their lamentations about the state of the country
  • In a viral clip, Kwam 1 slammed President Tinubu over the state of the country, noting that his policies were pushing too many Nigerians into misery and pain
  • Kwam 1's comment came as a shock to many because he was a big supporter of President Tinubu, who went around the country campaigning for him
  • Legit.ng recently spoke to Dr Azeez Abdulquadri, an associate professor of Political Science, about the state of the country in response to Kwam 1's video

Nigerian Fuji musician Wasiu Ayinde Marshal, aka Kwam1, recently made the headlines after a clip of him singing about the state of the nation went viral.

In the viral clip, Kwam 1 was heard calling his friend, President Tinubu, to order. He reminded him about the promises he made during his campaigns.

He noted that this was not what he told him nor promised Nigerians.

K1 noted in the viral clip that Nigerians are suffering, too many people are wallowing in misery, and if something isn't done quickly, people might be forced to hit the streets.

K1 warns Tinubu to the people's plights

In the viral clip, the veteran musician spoke about skyrocketing prices of foodstuff, petrol and other basic needs.

He also spoke about the insecurity bedevilling the country. K1 noted that kidnapping had become an evil bane that needed to be nipped in the bud.

"This is the beauty of democracy" - Dr Abdulquadri says

Days after Kwam 1's video where he lamented about the state of the economy went viral, Legit.ng spoke with Dr Abdulquadri an associate professor and a Labour Party stalwart. He said:

"To see Kwam1 an ardent supporter and follower of the President to come out and start lamenting it tells you a lot. However, I commend him for looking beyond his friendship to call the President to order. This is the beauty of democracy, we all feeling the pain."

Kwam1’s daughter wails over state of economy

Legit.ng recalls reporting a post shared online by Kwam1's daughter, Dami Marshal.

In her post, she called out the APC-led federal government, noting that the country's current state isn't what they promised Nigerians.

Dami bitterly lamented about the state of the economy, noting that things were in shambles and there was a need for drastic measures.

Meet Gogglebox star Pete Sandiford's wife and adorable family

The TV star, who appears on the show with his sister Sophie, shares two children with wife Paige

    Gogglebox stars Pete Sandiford and his sister Sophie are no doubt a fan favourite pairing on the Channel 4 reality show and are known for their witty one-liners that have us chuckling away every Friday night. 

    The sibling-duo often share glimpses of their personal lives in their conversations on the show, with Pete talking about his wife, Paige Yeomans, and their two children on several occasions. But who is Paige? Find out all we know about her here…

    While Pete has remained fairly tight-lipped about when he first met his wife, Paige, it was in 2020 when he broke the news that he had a girlfriend. 

    Sharing an adorable selfie of the couple, he wrote in the caption: "My Mrs and best mate what more could I ask for, don’t worry @sophiesandiford1 you are a close second best friend. This was taken last year FYI."

    Given the photo was taken in 2019, we can assume that the pair have been together for at least four years.

    Who is Pete's wife Paige Yeomans?

    While not much is known about Paige as she doesn't appear on , it has been reported that she is an emergency services call handler with a degree in English and linguistics from Queen Mary University of London.

    Pete revealed their engagement in 2021 on the Channel 4 show. In an episode that aired in February, Sophie turned to her brother, asking him if he felt "any different now that you've got engaged?" to which he responded: "Yeah, I think... I feel like an even bigger responsibility. I don't know why. I'm going to have a wife."

    The couple went on to tie the knot in May of the same year before welcoming their first child, Jimmy, in September. Watch Pete playing with his son in the sweet clip below. 

    Pete marked their six-month anniversary by sharing a stunning photo from their big day, showing the newlyweds lovingly beaming at one another while strolling down a boardwalk against an idyllic ocean backdrop.

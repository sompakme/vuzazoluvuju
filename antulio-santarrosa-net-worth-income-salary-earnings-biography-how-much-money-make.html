Template: a number of problems Antulio “Kobbo” Santarrosa is a puppeteer, television producer, journalist, comedian, actor, and author from Mayagüez, Puerto Rico. He is well known for his work as a journalist. Santarrosa was a producer and puppeteer on SuperXclusivo, which was the highest-rated television programme in the history of Puerto Rican television at the time of its airing. SuperXclusivo was removed off Puerto Rican television in the second half of 2012, allegedly as a result of a boycott initiated by the program’s sponsors and pressure from other political groups on the island. Three of Santarrosa’s puppets, La Cháchara (English: The Tittle-Tattler), La Condesa (English: The Countess), and La Comay (English: The Comay), are the most well-known (English: The Godmother). Additionally, Santarrosa is the author of Detrás de La Comay: biografa semi-autorizada (English: Behind The Godmother: Semi-authorized Biography—ISBN 5482700062), which is a semi-autobiographical work on the godmother Comay.

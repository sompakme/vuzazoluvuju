LA Knight Relentless In Pursuit of Roman Reigns With Scathing Promo at WWE Live Event

LA Knight made a significant impression after his debut at NXT TakeOver: Vengeance Day on February 14th, 2021. Knight lost to Roman Reigns at Crown Jewel, but that doesn’t mean he is done with Reigns, as he once again went after Reigns during a WWE live event.

LA Knight was unable to defeat Roman Reigns at WWE Crown Jewel last month, but he has been regularly featured on WWE television in a feud with Grayson Waller and Austin Theory.

During WWE’s Holiday Tour in Newark, Delaware, LA Knight and Kevin Owens squared off against Jimmy Uso and Solo Sikoa. Knight’s team won and then he cut a scathing promo on The Bloodline. Knight made it clear he will stop at nothing until he gets his hands on Roman Reigns again.

”I’ll give just a crumb, just a piece of what’s coming to The bloodline. I haven’t had enough until all of them fall and it’s just me and Roman Reigns with the WWE championship, when Reigns is staring up at the lights. It’s not an insult – just a fact of life.”

WWE also believes LA Knight has capped his popularity in the company and needs to develop a deeper connection with fans. Regardless, we’ll have to wait and see what’s next in store for LA Knight.

Record Of Ragnarok Chapter 91:- Record of Ragnarok is a popular manga series that features a battle between 13 gods and 13 humans to decide the fate of humanity. The manga is written by Shinya Umemura and Takumi Fukui and illustrated by Azychika. It has been serialized in Monthly Comic Zenon magazine since November 2017 and has 19 volumes as of October 2023. The manga has also been adapted into an anime series by Graphinica and Yumeta Company, which premiered on Netflix in June 2021 and January 2023 for the first and second seasons, respectively.

Recap of Record of Ragnarok Chapter 90

In the previous chapter of Record of Ragnarok, we saw the conclusion of the seventh round of the Ragnarok tournament, which featured the Greek god of the sun, Apollo, and the Chinese hero of the Three Kingdoms era, Liu Bei. The chapter showed how Liu Bei used his secret weapon, the Eight Geniuses, to counter Apollo’s overwhelming power. The Eight Geniuses were the eight loyal advisers and generals of Liu Bei, who sacrificed their lives to become his Volund, the Valkyrie Thrud.

With their help, Liu Bei was able to unleash a powerful attack called the Eight Formation, which pierced through Apollo’s solar armor and struck his heart. Apollo was shocked and impressed by Liu Bei’s courage and loyalty, and admitted his defeat before falling to the ground. Liu Bei then collapsed as well, having exhausted his life force. The chapter ended with the announcement of the winner of the seventh round: Liu Bei, the first human to defeat a god without dying.

Plotlines and Story of Record Of Ragnarok Chapter 91

The plot of Record of Ragnarok revolves around a fighting tournament between 13 gods and 13 humans, with the fate of humanity in the balance. The gods have decided to exterminate humankind, but the valkyries have proposed to give them one last chance to prove their worth. The valkyries have chosen 13 of the greatest heroes and warriors in human history, and have offered to become their Volund, or divine weapons, to fight against the gods.

The tournament consists of 13 rounds, and the first side to win seven rounds will be declared the victor. So far, six rounds have been completed, and the score is tied at 3-3. The seventh round has just ended, with Liu Bei winning against Apollo, giving humankind a slight advantage. The eighth round will begin soon, and the next pair of fighters will be revealed.

Expectations From This Chapter

The expectations for the next chapter are high, as fans are eager to see the eighth round of the Ragnarok tournament, which will feature Susanoo and Okita Souji. Susanoo is the Japanese god of storms, sea, and chaos, and the brother of Amaterasu and Tsukuyomi. He is known for his destructive and unpredictable nature, as well as his rivalry with his sister Amaterasu.

He is also the original owner of the Kusanagi-no-Tsurugi, one of the three sacred treasures of Japan. Okita Souji is the captain of the first unit of the Shinsengumi, the special police force of the Tokugawa shogunate. He is regarded as one of the best swordsmen in Japanese history, and is famous for his speed and skill with the katana.

He is also known for his loyalty to the Shinsengumi and his friendship with Kondo Isami and Hijikata Toshizo. He suffers from tuberculosis, which often affects his performance in battle. Fans are curious to see how these two fighters will clash, and what kind of Volund Okita will use. Fans are also intrigued by the spoiler that hints at another human fighter who will interfere in the battle, and wonder who it could be and what their motive is.

Ratings and Reviews

Record of Ragnarok is a popular manga series that has received positive ratings and reviews from critics and fans alike. The manga is praised for its epic fights, diverse characters, and creative twists on mythology and history. The manga has sold over 6 million copies as of June 2021, and ranked 10th in the 2021 Kono Manga ga Sugoi! (This Manga is Amazing!) list for male readers.

The manga has also been adapted into an anime series by Graphinica, which premiered on Netflix in June 2021. The anime adaptation has also been well-received, with some minor criticisms regarding its animation quality and pacing. The anime has a score of 8.09 out of 10 on MyAnimeList, and a 70% rating on Rotten Tomatoes. The anime has also been nominated for the Crunchyroll Anime Awards 2022 in the category of Best Fight Scene.

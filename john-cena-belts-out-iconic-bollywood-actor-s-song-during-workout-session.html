John Cena Belts Out Iconic Bollywood Actors Song During Workout Session

John Cena might be a future WWE Hall of Famer, but he also keeps an eye on everything else that happens outside of professional wrestling. He’s a solid actor in his own right and also notices other actors all over the world, especially in Bollywood. Therefore it’s no surprise he recently sang a legendary Bollywood actor’s song in a recent video.

The 16-time World Champion has a solid track record when it comes to his Hollywood movies, becoming one of the biggest success stories when it comes to a WWE Superstar transitioning to Hollywood.

One-half of the Bollywood Boyz Sunil Singh took to Twitter and uploaded a video of John Cena at the gym. The video started with Gurv introducing John Cena as a ‘‘’pretty big Shah Rukh Khan fan.’

Afterwards, Sunil Singh helped John Cena sing Shah Rukh Khan’s song Bholi Si Surat from his 1997 film Dil To Pagal Hai. Cena sang with a lot of gusto in Hindi, as it is likely fans in India were impressed by his singing skills.

John Cena’s ‘Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Mutant Mayhem’ also failed to win any awards at the Annie Awards recently. Nonetheless, Cena has a ton of appreciation for Shah Rukh Khan and fans will always love him for that.

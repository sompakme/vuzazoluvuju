Olivia Wilde Gets Real About Vinyl Nudity; Patrick Dempseys Beauty Moment The Hollywood

Olivia Wilde Discusses Wearing the Merkin on HBO’s Vinyl [YouTube]

Olivia Wilde has had to strip down on HBO’s Vinyl, but that’s not the part that makes her feel somewhat uncomfortable — it’s having to wear a merkin (aka fake pubic hair). “I was so vocal about it being fake because I was horrified that background actors might think this was actually my real body hair,” Wilde, clad in an H&M Conscious Collection dress, told Seth Meyers during his Late Night show on Thursday. Talk about a hairy situation.

Patrick Dempsey Is a Beauty Star in the Making [The Cut]

Attention Grey’s Anatomy fans: Patrick Dempsey has found a new gig. The actor now is serving as a beauty model for wife Jillian Dempsey. On Thursday, he posted an Instagram video of Jillian applying her new Lid Tints on his eyes. Though he doesn’t say anything in the video, he did caption: “I’m not a makeup guy, but I’m proud of my wife’s innovative product, Lid Tints for eyes.” What a good sport.

Topshop’s Latest Blog Post About Adele Backfires [Yahoo Style]

Topshop recently posted a blog praising Adele, but that didn’t bode well with the retailer’s fans. Instead, it caused folks to point out Topshop’s lack of sizing options and make comments such as, “Except you make no clothes that would fit her.” Yikes.

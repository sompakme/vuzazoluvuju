One Shot Tabakalera House Hotel Review, San Sebastin, Spain

San Sebastián, Basque Country, Spain

The 42-room One Shot Tabakalera House is tucked inside one of San Sebastián’s most vibrant buildings, and its irreverent attitude shines through from the décor to the toiletries. The thoroughly modern style is peppered with luxurious details, making it an appealing option right in the centre of town.

Corey Gamble mistaken for Kim Kardashians security see the awkward photo Wed 4 October 2

Corey Gamble was mistaken for Kim Kardashian’s ‘security’ as he posed with her and Kris Jenner at Paris Fashion Week. He wore matching black silk with his beau, while Kim stood out in her usual glamor in a silk pink dress. What does Corey Gamble actually do? It’s not security…

He was the only male to attend The Kardashians‘ Cabo vacation in season 4 episode 1. Corey Gamble is now posing with Kim Kardashian and her mom Kris Jenner, but fans are mistaking him for the reality TV star’s security guard. The famous family have been making their mark at Paris Fashion Week.

Corey Gamble mistaken for Kim’s security

Corey Gamble has been mistaken for Kim’s security in a series of pictures shared by Kris. A fan commented on the snaps, “Who is that guy with her? Security???”

Another jokily penned: “Is that Corry the CIA😂😂.” Kim often has security with her. Earlier this year, the star increased protection at her home in the gated community Hidden Hills, where Kylie Jenner lives.

“I heard Cory is the CIA😂😂😂😂,” a fellow fan wrote. It comes after Kanye West can be heard spewing some unconfirmed words towards Corey, whom he claims is in the “CIA” in a leaked video.

What does Corey Gamble do?

Corey was on a Justin Bieber tour as an assistant when Kris met him. Now he works at Jenner Communications but has been asked to take part in several projects, such as Yellowstone.

However, Kris told Corey “no” to appearing on the drama series. In the past, he has worked as a talent manager with Scooter Braun – the powerhouse agent behind Justin Bieber, Ariana Grande, and Demi Lovato.

He is an actor, business executive, and road manager. Corey’s net worth sits at a hefty $15 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. In February 2023, he debuted a silk collection with Dolce & Gabbana.

Kris Jenner and Kim at Paris Fashion Week

Kris Jenner and Kim Kardashian were seated next to the Beckhams at Paris Fashion Week. Kim turned up late and was originally sat next to Anna Wintour, who is believed to have snubbed her and moved.

Corey, in his usual silk attire, wore a silver watch and was seated next to his beau, Kris. He wore round sunglasses while Kim had on a silk gown with diamond jewelry at Victoria Beckham’s show.

Kim’s pink silk dress was made by the Spice Girls band member as part of her Spring 2024 collection. Jenner wore the black version, and added layered diamond necklaces, with a leather and shearling jacket.

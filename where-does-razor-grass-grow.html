Is pampas grass invasive?

Pampas grass is an invasive plant with a thicket of razor-sharp leaves that grows quickly and densely. It’s very tough to get rid of, and the plants’ dry regions are a fire threat. It may reach a height of eight to ten feet and produce plumes up to twelve feet long.

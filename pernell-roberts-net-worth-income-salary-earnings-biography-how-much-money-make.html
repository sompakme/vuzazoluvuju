Pernell Elven Roberts, Jr. (May 18, 1928 – January 24, 2010) was an American actor, singer, and musician who appeared on stage, in films, and on television. He was best remembered for his performances as Ben Cartwright’s oldest son Adam Cartwright on the Western television series Bonanza (1959–1965) and as head surgeon Dr. John McIntyre, the title character on Trapper John, M.D. (1979–1986). He also appeared as a guest actor on over 60 other shows. He was also well-known for his lifetime activism, which included his involvement in the Selma to Montgomery marches in 1965 and his efforts to persuade NBC to abstain from using white actors to play minorities in television shows.

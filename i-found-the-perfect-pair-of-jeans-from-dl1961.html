I Found the Perfect Pair of Jeans From DL1961

Finding the perfect pair of jeans can feel like an impossible task, but I finally hit the denim jackpot after trying a super comfy and flattering pair from the brand stars like Anne Hathaway, Meghan Markle, and Elsa Hosk wear on repeat: DL1961. 

The New York-based brand has been around since 2008, and celebrities have been devoted to its denim since then — Blake Lively has been spotted in its jeans as far back as the brand’s launch. It’s easy to see why these jeans are so universally loved; when I tried on DL1961’s Bridget High-Rise Bootcut Instasculpt pair, I immediately fell in love. They were the perfect fit, snug in all the right places, yet flared and airy at the bottom in a way that makes them a fun addition to any outfit. The love-at-first-try-on felt like a rare stroke of luck and inspired me to buy two pairs on the spot.

I usually reach for leggings or pajama pants when I want to kick back after work or during the weekend, but these jeans changed that for me; I find myself reaching for them when I’m looking for comfortable pants that add a little depth to an outfit. Oftentimes, you have to sacrifice comfort for a flattering style, opting for jeans that are stiff or tight in spots where you’d like a little more material, but these jeans are the exception. DL1961’s unique Instasculpt technology is intended to smooth, sculpt, and conform to your body for the perfect fit, but you don’t have to give up the comfort factor.

I didn’t even think it was possible for a pair of jeans to be this comfortable, but DL1961 converted me, and now I recommend the Bridget High-Rise Bootcut pair to everyone I know. Plus, I get compliments almost every time I wear them, and that feels rare and exciting for a pair of jeans. Afterall, they’re just jeans, right? Wrong. They’re the perfect pair of pants. The DL1961 Bridget High-Rise Bootcut Instasculpt jeans are just stretchy enough — so you won’t need to worry about unbuttoning them if they feel too tight at dinner — and easy to pair with a belt or wear on their own.

On top of the major comfort factor, these jeans have a versatile, bootcut silhouette that makes them ideal for any kind of shoe — whether you’re looking to wear sneakers or booties, or get more dressed up with heels or loafers. Some jeans are just a one-shoe-style wonder, but not this pair. They’re a great go-to for a day at the office, a date, or dinner with friends; they’re also a great pair of pants to rock if you’re just hosting people at home. I know it sounds crazy to hang out in jeans instead of pajamas or leggings, but yes, they’re that cozy.

Plus, DL1961 has plenty of styles to choose from. Along with the Bridget High-Rise Bootcut Instasculpt, DL1961 has many other silhouettes that have the Instasculpt feature, like relaxed, tapered boyfriend jeans and straight, low-rise jeans. I highly recommend trying on a few styles to see what lines up with your taste and body. That’s what I did, and it paid off.

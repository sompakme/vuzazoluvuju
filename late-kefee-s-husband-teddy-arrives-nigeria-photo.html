Late Kefee's Husband, Teddy, Arrives Nigeria (PHOTO)

Teddy Don-Momoh, the husband of the late Nigerian singer, Kefee, has landed in Nigeria for the burial ceremony of his wife.

A candle light session in honour of late popular singer Kefee Obareki Don Momoh takes place today, July 10, at Oba Akenzua Cultural centre in Benin, Edo State.

The branama crooner will be buried on Friday, July 11th in her hometown of Okpara-Inland, Ethiope East LGA of Delta State.

Kefee died of lungs failure on Thursday, June 12, 2014 after 15 days in coma at Desert Spring Hospital, Las Vegas.

Kefee and her husband, Teddy

Kefee's publicist in Lagos on Thursday released a statement stating her husband, Teddy Don-Momoh, who has been making arrangements to bring her body back from the U.S., would arrive the country first to receive her body before taking her down to Delta State for burial.

Kefee got married to Star FM On-Air-Personality, Teddy 'Don Momoh in 2012 and had in May celebrated her wedding anniversary.

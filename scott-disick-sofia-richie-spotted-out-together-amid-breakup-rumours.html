Scott Disick &amp; Sofia Richie Spotted Out Together Amid Breakup Rumours

The rumour mill went into overdrive after it was speculated that Scott Disick was canoodling with a mystery blonde at Kanye West's recent listening party in Wyoming. The sight of the Keeping Up with the Kardashians star wrapping his arms around another women would effectively lead one to assume that he and girlfriend Sofia Richie were no longer an item.

However, recent paparazzi photos suggest otherwise, as Disick and Richie were spotted out grocery shopping together Monday afternoon. The 19-year-old model was photographed wrapping hand around her 35-year-old boyfriend's arm, as the two fetched some iced-coffee together. The pair were also seen grabbing lunch at popular Japanese eatery Nobu, with sources noting how they were constantly smiling and looked particularly happy.

Disick recently addressed the widespread gossip that he and Richie have ended things. Taking to Instagram, he posted a Story that featured a tabloid headline claiming that the two have gotten back together, captioning the post with "we had no idea we even broke up but thanks for thinking of us."

Travis Scott Announces &quot;Rodeo&quot; Tour With Young Thug &amp; Metro Boomin

Before Travis Scott releases his highly anticipated debut album Rodeo, the G.O.O.D. Music rapper will be taking his talents on the road as part of the "Rodeo" tour alongside Young Thug and a DJ set from Metro Boomin.

The 23-date tour kick starts March 1st in Santa Ana, California and runs through cities like Denver, Chicago, L.A., Toronto, NYC, Houston, and of course Atlanta before wrapping up March 31st in Seattle. Tickets go on sale this Friday, while pre-sales go down tomorrow.

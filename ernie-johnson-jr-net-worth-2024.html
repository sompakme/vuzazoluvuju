Early Life and Career Beginnings

Ernie Johnson Jr. was born on August 7, 1956, in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. He is the son of Ernie Johnson Sr., a former Major League Baseball pitcher and Atlanta Braves play-by-play announcer.

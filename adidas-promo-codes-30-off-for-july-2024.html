FAQs

How can I get free shipping at Adidas?

Adidas has free shipping for all adiClub members. You can sign up for adiClub for free with your email address. Alternatively, standard shipping costs $4.99. 

How do I get a 15% off code at Adidas?

Sign up for adiClub and get 10-15% off your first purchase. Currently, Adidas will send you a unique 10% off welcome code although this has been 15% off in the past. Simply click on our relevant promo code or go to the Adidas website to sign up with your email address.

Does Adidas offer student discount?

Yes. Adidas now offers 15% off online or in-store with a valid student discount. You will need to log into UNiDAYS to confirm your student status and access your code.  

Does Adidas have a military discount?

Yes, Adidas gives 30% off military discount online and in-store. You can also get 15% off at Adidas outlet.You will need to verify your military status by using the ID.me form on the Adidas website. 

When are the Adidas sales?

Shop the sale all year round with the dedicated Adidas ‘sale’ page on its website. Our savings experts have previously seen discounts of up to 50% off with the Adidas mid-season sales on sportswear and athleisure.  

What is the returns policy at Adidas?

Returns can be made within 30 days of receipt to Adidas. Your items will need to be in their original condition and packaging, with any tags still attached. If you are part of the adiClub, returns are free of charge. 

Hints and tips

Seasonal sales: Shop the seasonal sales at Adidas to save on your next sportswear or sneakers purchase. Adidas is known to have end-of-season and mid-season sales with up to 50% off selected items. You can also save on key sales dates such as Black Friday, with previous discounts such as 70% off popular shoes. 

Adidas Membership: Sign up for the Adidas loyalty program—adiClub— to get free shipping on all your purchases. When you sign up, you’ll be able to earn points for your purchases. You can also earn points by using the Adidas running or training app, reviewing purchases, completing your profile, and sharing the app. The higher your loyalty tier, the better your rewards. For level one, you’ll get free shipping, early sale access, and a welcome discount.

Heroes discount: Teachers, first responders, medical professionals, nurses, and military personnel can all get 30% off their Adidas orders. This discount is valid both online and in-store. You will need to verify your status via ID.Me to be sent your unique code. There are some exclusions, such as celebrity collaborations, gift cards, limited edition Originals, or orders made via the Confirmed app.

Adidas apps: Download the Adidas app to get exclusive discounts and early sale notifications. You can also log into your Adidas account with the app to earn points on your purchases. Additionally, you can download the new Adidas Confirmed app for hyped new products and limited editions. Looking to get into fitness? The Adidas training or running apps can help you start and you can earn points too.

Are JoJo Siwa And Kim Kardashian Friends?

In a very rare crossover moment of pop culture, JoJo Siwa hung out with North West for a day — with Kim Kardashian's approval, of course. The YouTube video titled "BABYSITTING NORTH WEST!!! - JoJo Siwa" was uploaded to Siwa's channel — which has 11.9 million subscribers — in March 2019 and has gained 42 million views. In the nearly 7-minute video, Kardashian and North arrive at Siwa's home full of all things rainbow. Obviously growing fashion icon North arrived dressed in every color top to bottom, with a matching hair bow that came with its own train. 

Mommy Kim left North in the care of Siwa (and a large camera crew) to play on an in-home slide, dig into her famous big bow collection, play hide-and-seek, and make super glittery slime. In the end, the mother-of-four gave Siwa a hug and took the very excited North home. But what does Kardashian really think of Siwa?

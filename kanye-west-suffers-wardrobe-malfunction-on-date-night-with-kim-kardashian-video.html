Kanye West Suffers Wardrobe Malfunction on Date Night With Kim Kardashian (Video)

Fashion’s hot new couple, Kim Kardashian and Kanye West, stepped out for a date night on Friday at N62 Restaurant in Manhattan — where West gave new meaning to his song “It All Falls Down.”

As Kardashian trotted ahead, smiling demurely for the photogs, it was West who suffered from a wardrobe malfunction. While stepping out of their car outside the rapper’s apartment, West’s Balenciaga leather pants — which appeared to be secured by a studded belt — dropped dangerously low, revealing black briefs and some serious thigh.

West quickly realized the gaffe and pulled his pants up, but not before the paparazzi snapped a few quick pics. Kardashian, meanwhile, appeared unaware of the entire situation.

The Grammy Award-winner had much to celebrate on Friday, with three of his albums making the cut for Rolling Stone magazine’s coveted “500 Greatest Albums of All Time.” Late Registration, The College Dropout and My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy were among the 30 new titles added to the ranking, last updated in 2005.

Following their Friday night date, Kardashian jetted off to the nation’s capital to attend the White House Correspondents’ Dinner with mom Kris Jenner. But it was back to the Big Apple by Sunday, where the E! reality star attended a Broadway showing of the famous Wizard of Oz reimagining, Wicked.

“I just saw the play Wicked, it was soooo good! Everyone should def go see it!” Kardashian tweeted on Monday.

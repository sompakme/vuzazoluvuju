Braun Strowman Inching Closer to In-Ring Return After Neck Injury

Braun Strowman was one of the top stars on Friday Night SmackDown for the past year, until he was put on the shelf due to injury. He had surgery for his neck afterwards and has been rehabbing ever since. Now Strowman has given some positive news regarding his recovery progress.

The Monster Among Monsters had to undergo fusion neck surgery to fix his longstanding injuries back in June last year. Since then, Strowman’s been on a mission to nurse himself back to full health while keeping busy with other activities.

As previously mentioned, Braun Strowman has been given the green light to start weight training again after his surgery. Naturally, he has been making good use of his newfound freedom.

Braun Strowman took to Twitter and shared an Instagram post about his injury recovery progress, making it clear that he is getting closer and closer to a return to the squared circle.

”Closer and closer. The Monster’s Coming Home!!!!”

Braun Strowman also has a huge opponent in mind for his WWE return. We will have to wait and see when Strowman will be able to make his triumphant return to WWE television, as fans certainly miss him already.

15 Best Hairstyles With Bangs

Felicity Jones

Getty Images

Felicity Jones's graduated bang gets longer at the temples, drawing attention straight to her high cheekbones.

Michelle Williams

Getty Images

This wouldn't be Hollywood's most perfect pixie without an off-center part and soft, side-swept bangs.

Rihanna

Getty Images

Rihanna keeps her grown-up lob looking modern and edgy with a tousled texture and long, choppy fringe.

Jessica Biel

Getty Images

Jessica Biel adds sophistication to long, textured locks with smooth, straight fringe that hit at eyebrow level. The result? Youthful yet refined.

Jennifer Lopez

Getty Images

Sexy bombshell bangs require tons of volume, lots of length, and subtle highlights á la Jennifer Lopez.

Reese Witherspoon

Getty Images

Bangs have become this star's signature. Her latest iteration is also the easiest to maintain: brushed to the side over one eye.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Alessandra Ambrosio

Getty Images

The Brazilian beauty snips her supermodel strands into a barely-there bang that feels as wispy and weightless as couture feathers.

Taylor Swift

Getty Images

Taylor Swift proves curly girls can wear bangs—with the help of a frizz-busting serum or gel, of course. Take a spiraled shag from day to night by running over your natural texture with a mini flat iron. 

Emma Stone

Getty Images

So they don't distract from her undone updo, Emma Stone tucks her bangs behind one ear.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Solange

Getty Images

Solange Knowles shows off her bold, born-with-it texture—cool, curly bangs, included.

Christina Hendricks

Getty Images

All types of bangs enhance heart-shaped faces. Christina Hendricks opts for a softly snipped version separated at the center of the forehead.  

Lea Michele

Getty Images

When you want to soften bluntly-cut bangs, simply separate them in the center for a feathery effect. Lea Michele's look is dramatic without stealing the spotlight from her features.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Mandy Moore

Getty Images

This is how to accomplish a graceful grow out. Mandy Moore's bangs graduate to soft, cheekbone-grazing angles, blending perfectly into her nonchalant waves. 

Laura Dern

Getty Images

On occasions when you wish your bangs out of the way— take note of Laura Dern's technique. Style them to one side then use a strong-hold hairspray to ensure they stay there.    

Lena Dunham

Getty Images

The baby bang suits Lena Dunham's trendy style and wavy lob. Pro-tip: keep your mini flat iron handy, for micro fringe is best worn sleek and straight.

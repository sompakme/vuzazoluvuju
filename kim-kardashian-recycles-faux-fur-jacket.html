Kim Kardashian recycles faux fur jacket

    Kim Kardashian is rarely seen in the same outfit twice, however the 34-year-old fashionista was spotted rocking a stylish faux-fur coat on two separate outings in the space of a two days. The star wore the oversized grey coat as she made a fleeting visit to New York to surprise make-up artist friend Mario Dedivanovic, who was giving a talk.

    Kim Kardashian was spotted recycling a faux fur coat

    Rapper husband Kanye West did not join her, however Kim was not alone as she spent the rest of the trip enjoying some quality time with close friend Jonathan Cheban. For her first look, Kim chose to keep it casual with a plain black t-shirt and leggings, teamed with black leather boots. The mother-of-one wore her hair slicked back into a sleek ponytail, and accessorised her outfit with a pair of oversized hoop earrings. Renowned for her fashion-forward ensembles, when Kim headed back to the airport the following day, she chose a more glamorous look, pairing the coat with a black bodycon dress which featured some bejewelled embellishment.

    Kim paid a fleeting visit to New York to surprise a friend

    Once again Kim chose to wear her hair in a slicked back ponytail, although this time she wore no jewellery, finishing off the look with a pair of black leather boots.

    Fashionistas around the world are always looking to see what Kim will wear next, but the American beauty recently admitted that she and Kanye are working on a new 'vision' for her style.

    "Up all night redoing my whole wardrobe with Kanye," she tweeted earlier this month. "I'm so ready for new looks for the New Year! New 2015 vision."

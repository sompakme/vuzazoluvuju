About Lil Dicky:

Lil Dicky, whose real name is David Andrew Burd, is an American artist known for his work in rapping, comedy, and acting. He grew up in the suburbs of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, and attended Richmond Robins School. He later graduated summa cum laude from the University of Richmond with a major in business. Initially working in marketing, Lil Dicky transitioned to music, gaining fame with his viral music video “Ex-Boyfriend” in 2013. His career has since included a successful debut album “Professional Rapper,” acting roles, and philanthropic efforts.

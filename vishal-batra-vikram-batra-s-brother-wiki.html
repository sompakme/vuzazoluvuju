Vishal Batra’s Biography, Wiki (Birthday & Education)

Bio – Vishal Batra took birth on 09 September 1974 in Palampur, Himachal Pradesh, India. He was born few minutes before his twin brother Vikram Batra. Let me tell you that Vishal is 46 years old as of 2020. 

According to his IMDB profile, he has attended Kendriya Vidyalaya, Palampur for his senior secondary education. In 1992, he enrolled himself in the University Campus, Summer hill, Shimla for his Bachelor’s degree in Commerce. 

After completing his graduation, Batra studied for a Master’s in Finance & Control (MFC) in 1996. He and his brother participated in many competitions in his school and college time.

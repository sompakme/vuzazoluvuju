Miro Addresses Speculation of Separation from Lana

Miro went to AEW for better opportunities for himself and for some time, that is exactly what he got when Miro became the TNT Champion. That said, his good times in AEW didn’t last long and was eventually relegated to a long hiatus from the company. While he remains absent from AEW, Miro decided to mock a fan who claimed he and Lana are separated.

Miro has not been seen in AEW since All Out, and he has not even competed on Dynamite for several months. This is a huge cause of concern for many fans, as they feel Miro is simply being wasted entirely in the company and deserves better.

As previously reported, Miro returned to Bulgaria while ago and is still sitting at home as AEW simply does not have anything for him. His continued absence has only led to a rise in rumors.

Miro is happily married to Lana and they remain a beloved couple. However, a fan took to Twitter and claimed Miro and Lana are separated. Miro replied to the fan and smoked him by referencing his love triangle storyline with Bobby Lashley and Lana.

Didn’t you know she is with Bobby Lashley still

There is also the belief that Tony Khan might feel pushing Miro might be waste. The former Rusev wants to win the AEW World Title in the future. Regardless, we’ll have to see what the future holds for Miro when it comes to his status in AEW.

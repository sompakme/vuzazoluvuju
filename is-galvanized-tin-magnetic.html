What is the best way to tell whether something is tin?

Is tin an uncommon metal?

Native tin (the latin word for tin is stannum, which gives tin its chemical symbol Sn) is an uncommon mineral that can only be found in small quantities. It is so seldom encountered that it cannot be considered a tin ore in any sense. Pure tin metal has limited applications, and as a result, the majority of tin is found in alloys. Bronze is the most well-known of all tin alloys.

Dont Have Singles As Bestie to the Extent of Being Your Hubbys Friend: Tonto Dikeh Warns

  • Popular actress and brand influencer Tonto Dikeh addressed the issue of letting single women get too much comfort in one’s home
  • The mother of one cautioned married women to be careful how they let their single friends become friends with their husbands
  • Tonto added that married women should protect their homes and their joys from their adversaries

Popular Nigerian actress Tonto Dikeh has taken to social media to caution married women against certain relationships with their single friends.

The movie star said married women should not let their female friends get so close to the point of being friends with their husbands.

Tonto explained that individuals should learn to hold themselves to a standard of perfection and added that some people are not accustomed to working in settings where excellence is demanded.

This is coming following the trending report of an alleged relationship between celebrity stylist, Mimi Linda Yina aka Medlin Boss, and her best friend, Nimi Nwofor’s husband.

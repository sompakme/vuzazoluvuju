Ikorodu Bois Recreate 21 Savage, Lildurk and Nardo Wicks Song With Wheelbarrow, Mop Head, Hilario

  • A talented group of youngsters, Ikorodu Bois, have come through with their craft again as they dropped their version of an international song
  • The young boys who are known to recreate music videos with basic everyday items as props jumped on 21 Savage, Lildurk and Nardo Wick's Who Want Smoke
  • Ikorodu Bois wore oversized shirts, coats and employed other props such as their famous wheelbarrow and a mop head

Popular Internet sensation, Ikorodu Bois, in their usual fashion dropped their version of the music video of the song Who Want Smoke by international artistes, 21 Savage, Lildurk and Nardo Wick.

The boys made use of an uncompleted building as well as the roadside to bring their piece to life as they donned oversized shirts and coats.

Improvising for cars and other expensive gadgets, Ikorodu Bois had their wheelbarrow and an iron carriage.

What exactly is batter board in the building industry?

In construction, batter boards (also known as battre boards, and often mispronounced as “batter boads”) are temporary frameworks that are placed beyond the corners of a planned foundation at certain elevations. These batter boards are then used to hold layout lines (construction twine) that are utilised to mark the foundation’s limitations (edges and corners).

Is it necessary for batter boards to be level?

When constructing a home or structure, batter boards are used to indicate the framework during the first excavation phase of the construction. Using batter boards, you may verify that all of the foundation walls are constructed at the proper angles. In the event that they are not squared and level, they may cause your foundation, and therefore your home, to be off of level.

Who plays Winnie Peterson in Liar season 2?

Liar’s Winnie Peterson is played by 36-year-old British actress Amy Nutall.

Originally from Lancashire, Amy trained at performing arts school and went on to become an actress and singer, with her debut album Best Days reaching number four on the UK classical charts.

Amy now works a TV actress and is married to Broadchurch actor Andrew Buchanan with whom she has a 4-year-old child.

Amy Nuttall at the BAFTA TV Awards 2013

What else has she starred in?

Amy’s most notable role to date was playing Ethel Parks in the hit ITV period drama, Downton Abbey.

However, she first came onto our screens playing Chloe Atkinson in Emmerdale, before going on to have various parts in Hotel Babylon, The Musketeers and Death in Paradise.

Amy’s performance as housemaid Ethel on Downton Abbey saw her receive a Screen Actor’s Guild award, for ‘Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama Series’ in 2013.

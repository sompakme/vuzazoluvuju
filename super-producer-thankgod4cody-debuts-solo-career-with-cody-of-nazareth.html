Super Producer ThankGod4Cody Debuts Solo Career With &quot;Cody of Nazareth&quot;

25-year old Memphis bred producer ThankGod4Cody launches first musical and lyrical efforts with the release of the project Cody of Nazareth. The producer acquired industry cred via his role as the production behind major hits such as SZA's "Love Galore" and "The Weekend." And although the musical prodigy already garnered much respect, a fact supported by his 3 Grammy nominations and a 7x platinum distinction, he continues to impress in versatility. The 11-track project includes no features, but offers a unique blend of trap, hip hop on tracks "Emotional" or "Deceived" as well as delicate vibes on tunes like "WYA."

Upon the project's release, the talented producer immediately received a co-sign from SZA who applauded his efforts through an Instagram shout out. The TDE-signed muse quotes "Ummm the genius behind love galore the weekend and broken clocks just put out his OWN project !! Super proud of you dookie ☺️ !! CODY OF NAZARETH OUT NOW on all platforms."

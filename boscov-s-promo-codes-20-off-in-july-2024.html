FAQs

Does Boscov’s have free shipping?

Boscov’s does charge shipping fees for most of its items. However, you can often get free shipping promotions on selected totals from Boscov’s. Currently, you can qualify for free shipping on orders of $69 or more. Alternatively, check this page for occasional free shipping Boscov’s coupons.  

How can I get Boscov’s rewards?

You will need to sign up for a Boscov’s credit card to start earning rewards on your purchases. Once you get to 500 points, you can redeem a $5 credit towards your next Boscov’s purchase. You’ll also get 15% off your first day of purchasing when you spend within 30 days of opening your account.  

Does Boscov’s have a military discount?

Yes, Boscov’s has a 15% off military discount. You can even use this discount during sales events. To get this discount, simply complete the ‘military discount’ form on Boscov’s website.  

When are the Boscov’s sales?

Save even more on Boscov’s high-quality products all year round with sales events. You can shop by department and choose the ‘clearance’ page to view strong discounts on items reduced to clear. Key times to shop are also during Black Friday sales, with previous discounts of up to 75% off.  

What is the return policy at Boscov’s?

Returns for most items can be made within 90 days at Boscov’s. Your items will need to be in their original condition and packaging with any tags still attached. Selected items such as jewelry, mattresses, furniture, patio, sofas, and air-con have different return periods so be sure to double-check these from Boscov’s website.  

Hints and tips

Newsletter discounts: Sign up for Boscov’s email newsletter to get the latest sales and promotions sent to you. To sign up, go to Boscov’s website footer and type your email address. You’ll also be kept updated with the latest brand news and new products. 

Text sign-up: You can also sign up for text updates from Boscov and get a 10% off coupon in return. This discount can only be used for first-time subscribers. Additionally, you cannot use this discount on selected brands or in conjunction with additional promotions. 

Boscov’s Black Friday sales: Shop the Black Friday sales at Boscov’s to save on clothing, furniture, and jewelry. Each November the brand usually runs these sales, with previous discounts of up to 80% off selected items. If you can’t wait until then, check the ‘clearance’ page for some smart savings.

Promotional weekly ads: Are you new to Boscov’s website? You might not realize but there is a ‘weekly ad’ page where you can save with even more promotions.  This page has the layout of a magazine, sales dates for your diary, and models wearing Boscov’s discounted clothing. 

Boscov’s Credit Card: If you are a regular shopper at Boscov, you might consider a Boscov credit card. When you sign up, you’ll automatically join Be Rewarded, which is Boscov’s loyalty program. You’ll also be sent 15% off your first purchase. With every $1 spent, you’ll get 2 points or 4 points with cosmetics and fragrance products. When you earn 500 points, this can be used for a $5 voucher reward at Boscov’s.

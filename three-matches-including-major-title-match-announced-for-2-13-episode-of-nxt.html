Three Matches Including Major Title Match Announced For 2/13 Episode Of NXT

Tonight’s episode of NXT featured some of the fallout from NXT: Vengeance Day. We got an explanation for why Carmelo Hayes betrayed Trick Williams. Bron Breakker and Baron Corbin were also in a celebratory mood tonight after winning the Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic.

After Breakker and Corbin called out the Family, Tony D’Angelo, and Channing “Stacks” Lorenzo came out and suggested they face off in a title match on the 2/13 episode of WWE NXT.

Noam Dar was challenged by Von Wagner a couple of weeks ago. While Von Wagner put on a good performance, Dar did everything to retain the Heritage Cup. Hence, Wagner and Robert Stone are not done with Meta Four. Next week on NXT, Wagner and Stone will face off against Noam Dar and Oro Mensah.

Tonight on NXT, Brinley Reece was embarrassed backstage by Kiana James when she had her coffee poured into a trash can. Next week on NXT, Reece will have her chance for revenge when she faces Kiana James.

WWE NXT Lineup (2/13/2024):

NXT Tag Team Championship: The Family (c) vs. Bron Breakker & Baron Corbin

Noam Dar & Oro Mensah vs. Von Wagner & Robert Stone

Kiana James vs. Brinley Reece

So far, the 2/13 episode of WWE NXT is shaping up to be an exciting episode. The most anticipated match of the night will be the NXT Tag Team Championship. Stay tuned to Ringside News for live coverage of WWE NXT.

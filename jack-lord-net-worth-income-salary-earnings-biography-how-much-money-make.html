In his stage name, Jack Lord, John Joseph Patrick Ryan (December 30, 1920 – January 21, 1998) was an American actor who appeared in television shows, films and on Broadway. He died on January 21, 1998, at the age of 8He was best remembered for his prominent role as Steve McGarrett in the American television series Hawaii Five-O, which aired from 1968 until 1980 on the CBS network. Earlier in his career, Lord acted in a number of feature films, including Man of the West (1958). In Dr. No, the first James Bond film, he was the first actor to portray the recurrent role Felix Leiter, who was played by him.

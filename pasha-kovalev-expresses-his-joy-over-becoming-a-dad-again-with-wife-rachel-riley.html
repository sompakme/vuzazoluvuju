Pasha Kovalev expresses his joy over becoming a dad again with wife Rachel Riley

The Strictly stars are due to welcome their second baby in autumn

    Former star Pasha Kovalev has broken his silence following the news that he and his wife Rachel Riley are to welcome their second child.

    The new addition, who is due in autumn, will be a sibling for the couple's 16-month-old daughter Maven.

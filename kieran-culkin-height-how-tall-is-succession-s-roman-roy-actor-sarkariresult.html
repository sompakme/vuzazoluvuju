Transitioning into his teenage years, Culkin impressed audiences and critics alike with standout performances in both indie hits and mainstream films. His depiction of the flawed and privileged COO not only won widespread acclaim but also fetched him a Golden Globe Award, a Critics’ Choice Television Award, and three Primetime Emmy Award nominations.

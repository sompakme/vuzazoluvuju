Heavy Metal &amp; Reflective

Azealia Banks seemed doomed to be yet another major label casualty, but the rapper wisely (and luckily) parted ways with Universal earlier this month, and today we've been given her first release as an independent artist.

Azealia described the song on twitter, saying “‘HEAVY METAL AND REFLECTIVE’ is an ASS-CLAPPERRRRRRRR !!!!!” she said. “All the big butts, little butts, fake butts and real butts gonna be clapping for their LIVESSSSS.”

The video has already been filmed, and will debut on August 5th, while the song will be available for purchase on iTunes tomorrow. Looks like we'll finally get to hear Broke With Expensive Taste soon. Y'all feelin' this new one?

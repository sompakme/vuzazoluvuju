Daniel Padilla (born 26 April 1995, Age: 28 years old) is a Filipino actor and recording artist who has been dubbed as the “Teen King” and the “King of Hearts” by his fans and the media. He is one of the most popular and successful young stars in the Philippines, with numerous awards and accolades to his name. He is also known for his on-screen and off-screen partnership with actress Kathryn Bernardo, collectively known as “KathNiel”.

Early Life and Education

Daniel was born on April 26, 1995, in Manila, Philippines. His full name is Daniel John Elago Ford Padilla. He is the son of actors Rommel Padilla and Karla Estrada, who separated when he was a child. He has seven half-siblings from his father’s side and three half-siblings from his mother’s side. He is also the nephew of action star Robin Padilla and former actor BB Gandanghari.

Padilla grew up with his mother and siblings in Tacloban, Leyte, and later moved to Quezon City. He attended O.B. Montessori Center and then enrolled in the home study program of UST Angelicum College. He was interested in show business from a young age, as he often accompanied his mother to her shoots and events.

Career

Padilla started his acting career in 2010, when he was scouted by a talent agent who introduced him to Star Magic, the talent management arm of ABS-CBN. He made his television debut with a supporting role in the soap opera Gimik 2010, before landing the lead role in Growing Up in 2011. He gained more recognition and popularity when he starred in the hit television series Princess and I (2012-2013), alongside Kathryn Bernardo, Enrique Gil, and Khalil Ramos.

Padilla and Bernardo became one of the most sought-after love teams in the Philippines, as they starred in several more TV series, such as Got to Believe (2013-2014), Pangako Sa ‘Yo (2015-2016), La Luna Sangre (2017-2018), and 2 Good 2 Be True (2022). Their TV shows consistently ranked among the most-watched programs in the country and earned them various awards and nominations.

Padilla also ventured into film, making his debut as a supporting actor in 24/7 in Love and Sisterakas (both 2012). He then starred in several box-office hit films with Bernardo, such as Must Be… Love, Pagpag: Siyam na Buhay (both 2013), She’s Dating the Gangster (2014), Crazy Beautiful You (2015), Barcelona: A Love Untold (2016), Can’t Help Falling in Love (2017), and The Hows of Us (2018). Padilla’s films have grossed over ₱3.14 billion in the box office, with the lattermost becoming the highest-grossing Filipino film of all time.

As a recording artist, Padilla has released five albums under Star Music, namely Daniel Padilla (2012), DJP (2013), I Heart You (2014), I Feel Good (2015), and the compilation album DJ’ Greatest Hits (2016). All of his albums have achieved double platinum status in the Philippines. He has also held solo concerts, such as Daniel Live! (2013), DOS (2014), Most Wanted (2015), and DJ Classics: Back at the Araneta (2017).

Padilla is also an endorser of various brands and products, such as Pepsi, BUM Equipment, Bench, Nescafe, Honda, and Smart Communications. He is also a philanthropist, supporting causes such as education, environment, and disaster relief.

Separation with Kathryn Bernardo

One of the most popular and beloved celebrity couples in the Philippines, Kathryn Bernardo and Daniel Padilla, also known as KathNiel, have officially confirmed their breakup after 11 years of being together. He taught me how to love, how to dream, how to grow, and how to become a better person. He was my best friend, my partner, my protector, and my home.”

“But as we both enter a new phase in our lives, we have realized that we are heading in different directions, and that staying together might not be the best thing for us anymore. “This is not a goodbye, but a see you again. I will always be here for him, as a friend and as a fan. And I hope he will be there for me too. I hope you will continue to support us individually, as we pursue our own paths and dreams. Thank you for being part of our love story. We love you all.”

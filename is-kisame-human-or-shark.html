Is Kisame human or shark?

Kisame was a very tall, muscular shinobi, and was the tallest member in Akatsuki. He had a distinctive shark-like appearance, complete with pale, green skin in the manga, (depicted as blue-grey in the anime).

Is Kisame a part shark?

Kisame fits this original monstrous idea as well. He is a towering shark person who would easily fit the description of a monster. When Kisame fuses with Samehada, he becomes even more monstrous, growing a tail and fins that make him look like a sea monster from a horror movie.

What species is Kisame?

"Kisame" means demon shark, while "Hoshigaki" may mean dried persimmon. Kishimoto first intended to make Akatsuki's members monsters with almost no human traits. Zetsu, Kisame and Kakuzu are prime examples of this.

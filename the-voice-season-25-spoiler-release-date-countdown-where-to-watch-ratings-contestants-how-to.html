The Voice Season 25 Spoiler, Release Date, Countdown, Where to Watch, Ratings, Contestants &amp; How to

The Voice Season 25:- The Voice, NBC’s hit singing competition show, is returning for its landmark 25th season in spring 2024. The show has been on the air for more than 12 years, and has featured a-list coaches ranging from Miley Cyrus to CeeLo Green, who have given their expertise to hundreds of hopeful singers.

The show’s innovative format features four stages of competition: the blind auditions, the battle rounds, the knockouts, and the live performance shows. The Voice Season 25 is expected to be one of the most thrilling seasons yet, as it will introduce some new twists and surprises. Here is everything you need to know about The Voice Season 25.

Release Date & Time

The Voice Season 25 will premiere on Monday, February 26, 2024, at 8/7c on NBC. The second premiere episode will air on Tuesday, February 27, 2024, at 8/7c on NBC. The show will then air Mondays and Tuesdays on NBC at 8/7c and next day on Peacock. The season finale is expected to air sometime in May 2024. As of December 5, 2023, there are 448 days left until the premiere of The Voice Season 25.

Spoiler Alert

The Voice Season 25 will mark the first time a coaching duo has graced the panel. That means two people will technically occupy one of the four total coach slots. The coaching duo for Season 25 is country duo Dan + Shay, Dan Smyers and Shay Mooney, who have won multiple Grammy Awards and have collaborated with artists like Justin Bieber and Kelly Clarkson. They will be joined by returning coaches Reba McEntire, John Legend, and Chance the Rapper.

McEntire is a country legend who has sold over 75 million records worldwide and has won 16 ACM Awards, 15 American Music Awards, 9 People’s Choice Awards, 6 CMA Awards, 3 Grammy Awards, and a Kennedy Center Honor. Legend is a singer, songwriter, producer, and activist who has won 11 Grammy Awards, an Academy Award, a Golden Globe Award, a Tony Award, and an Emmy Award.

He is also the founder of the Show Me Campaign, which works to end poverty and improve education. Chance the Rapper is a rapper, singer, songwriter, and philanthropist who has won 3 Grammy Awards and has donated millions of dollars to Chicago public schools and mental health services.

Storyline & Plot

The storyline for Season 25 will follow the same format as previous seasons, with the coaches searching for the best voices in America and mentoring them to become artists. The coaches will have to compete with each other to recruit the most impressive singers to their teams during the blind auditions, where they can only hear the singers’ voices and not see their faces. The coaches will also have the power to steal singers from other teams during the battle rounds and the knockouts, where the singers will face off against each other in duets and solos.

The coaches will also have the option to use their one-time save to bring back one of their eliminated singers during the knockouts. The live performance shows will feature the top singers from each team performing for the viewers’ votes, and the coaches will also have the chance to save one of their singers from elimination each week. The viewers will ultimately decide which singer will be worthy of the grand prize, which is a recording contract and $100,000.

Ending Explanation

The ending of The Voice Season 25 will depend on the viewers’ votes and the coaches’ decisions. The final four singers from each team will compete in the finale, where they will perform a solo song, a duet with their coach, and an original song. The viewers will vote for their favorite singer, and the singer with the most votes will be crowned the winner of The Voice Season 25.

The runner-up, the third-place, and the fourth-place singers will also be announced. The winner will receive a recording contract and $100,000, and the opportunity to work with their coach and other industry professionals to launch their music career.

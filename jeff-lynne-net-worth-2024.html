Early Life and Career Beginnings

Jeff Lynne was born in Birmingham, England, and developed a passion for music at a young age. He began his career as a member of the band “The Idle Race,” before joining “The Move” and eventually forming “Electric Light Orchestra” (ELO). His unique blend of rock and classical music quickly gained popularity, setting the stage for a successful career.

Summer House: News, TV show episodes and spoilers

Is Winter House and Summer House connected?

The success of Summer House led to the spin-off Winter House and Summer House: Martha's Vineyard.

What original cast members are still in Summer House?

The cast has changed a lot since the start of the Bravo show, but Carl, Kyle and Lindsay are still on the series.

Is Summer House scripted?

While some rumors believed the show was fake, the people and events are real. But like other Bravo reality shows, there may be moments that are suggested by crew, but there is no set script.

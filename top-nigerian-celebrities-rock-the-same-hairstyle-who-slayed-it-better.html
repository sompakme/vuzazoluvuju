Top Nigerian celebrities rock the same hairstyle, who slayed it better?

- Nigerian top celebrities have been spotted rocking similar hairstyles

- Tonto Dikeh, Annie Idibia, and Adesua Etomi-Wellington rocked the hairstyle, finger waves, first

- The celebrities were later joined by Big Brother Naija winner 2019 Mercy Eke

Hair is beautiful, it defines the look of a woman. Every hairstyle changes how a woman looks. It has often been a joke among men that women who rock different hairstyles often look different with every style they choose.

Top Nigerian celebrities, Annie Idibia, Tonto Dikeh, Mercy Eke, and Adesua Etomi-Wellington have been seen rocking similar hairstyles at different times. This particular hairstyle is known as finger waves.

It can be achieved with natural hair and also with a weave or wig. Finger waves style is achieved by curling hair into waves.
